Tabernacle Show - Les petites Mouettes

Watch part of our performance on YouTube

Bach: Prelude in C Major, BWV 846

Watch on YouTube

The first prelude from Bach's Well-Tempered Clavier Book I, this piece features a beautiful progression of arpeggiated chords that create a serene atmosphere.

Performed on a Steinway grand piano at the winter recital in December 2023.

Original Composition: "Autumn Reflections"

Listen on YouTube

An original piano composition inspired by the changing colors and moods of autumn. This piece combines elements of impressionist harmonies with contemporary rhythmic patterns.

Composed in October 2023 and premiered at the Student Composers' Concert.
